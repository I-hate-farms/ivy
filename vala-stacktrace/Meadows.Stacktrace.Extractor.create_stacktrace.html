create_stacktrace

Description:

public void create_stacktrace (Stacktrace trace)

Populate the stacktrace with frames extracted from Linux.Backtrace and completed via calls to unix tools `nm` and `addr2line`

Warning:

because this methods creates synchronously other processes (nm and addr2line), it can a significant impact on performance

Parameters:

trace

the stacktrace
